Associate Professor of Civil Engineering

I am a faculty member in the Civil Engineering program at Quinnipiac University. As a professor in a teaching-oriented institution, I teach courses in structural and materials engineering.

My teaching interests are in structural analysis, mechanics of materials, reinforced concrete design, and steel design. My research interests are aimed at understanding the behavior of porous civil engineering materials, including deterioration mechanisms in cement and mortar, microstructural modeling, cement characterization, repair methods for damaged concrete structures, and the development of sustainable civil engineering materials. I am also interested in engineering education research, especially methods on how to effectively integrate writing in the engineering curriculum.
